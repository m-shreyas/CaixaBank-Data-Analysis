---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🔹 Monthly Spend Trend
- Steady growth from January through April
- Sharp, unexpected decline in May — flagged as a business risk

### 🔹 Transaction Volume Analysis
- Volume drop identified as the **primary driver** of revenue decline
- Average transaction value remained stable → the problem is frequency, not spending intent

### 🔹 Customer Behavior
- Active customer count increased steadily throughout the year
- Engagement frequency declined — customers retained but transacting less

---

## 🧠 Business Interpretation

The data tells a clear story: this bank has a **retention problem, not an acquisition problem.**

- ✅ Strong customer retention — headcount growing
- ⚠️ Declining transaction frequency — engagement at risk
- ❓ May anomaly requires investigation — potential seasonal or behavioral trigger

---

## ✅ Recommendations

1. **Investigate the May decline** — rule out data issues, then identify behavioral triggers
2. **Launch engagement initiatives** — loyalty programs, personalized offers
3. **Track transaction volume weekly** — monthly granularity misses early warning signals
